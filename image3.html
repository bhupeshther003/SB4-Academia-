Demo: Create Chatbot using IBM Watson Assistant

Step 1: Log in to your IBM Cloud account.
Step 2: Search for IBM Watson Assistant service.
Step 3: Select Lite Plan, check the checkbox and click Create.
Step 4: Click on Launch Watson Assistant.
Step 5: Enter assistant name and description.
Step 6: Choose Other plan and click Next.
Step 7: Enter chatbot name, choose colors and image.
Step 8: Click Create.
Step 9: Click Action button on left side.
Step 10: Click Create Action.
Step 11: Define greeting messages like Hi, Hello, etc.
Step 11a: Click Conversation step bar.
Step 12: Enter assistant response for greeting.
Step 13: Ask for consumer name and define response as free text.
Step 14: Set variable for name.
Step 15: Enter variable name as 'name' and type free text.
Step 16: Select action step variable in To option.
Step 17: Add assistant variable using fx icon.
Step 18: Click Save and close.
Step 19: Create new action for questions.
Step 20: Add consumer question like course list.
Step 21: Provide course options and responses.
Step 22: Add all courses in option list.
Step 23: Create conditions for each course.
Step 24: Save action and preview chatbot.
Step 25: Create action for course enrollment.
Step 26: Ask qualification with Yes/No options.
Step 27: Ask contact number after Yes response.
Step 28: Confirm admission callback within 24 hours.
Step 29: Preview chatbot and verify responses.
Step 30: Publish chatbot.
Step 31: Click Create Version.
Step 32: Integrate chatbot with channels like Twilio.
Step 33: Select Twilio integration.
Step 34: Create or setup Twilio account.
Step 35: Copy SID and Auth token from Twilio.
Step 36: Click Finish.
Step 37: Open WhatsApp with Twilio.
Step 38: Open WhatsApp again with Twilio.
Step 39: Fill required details.
Step 40: Test WhatsApp messaging from Twilio.
Step 41: Scan QR code and send join message to activate chatbot.
